100 prisoners are numbered 1 to 100 and forced to enter a room one at a time. They are not allowed to communicate with the other prisoners after they go in. The room contains 100 closed boxes labeled 1 to 100 on the outside. 100 pieces of paper are labeled 1 to 100 and randomly placed inside each box so that they can't be removed. Therefore, most of the boxes contain numbers which don't coincide with their outside numbers. Each prisoner is allowed to open no more than 50 boxes to find their number. If each of the 100 prisoners finds their number, they are all spared. If even one prisoner does not find their own number, they all will be executed. Assuming the prisoners can discuss it beforehand, what strategy can they execute to give them a 30% chance of survival?
